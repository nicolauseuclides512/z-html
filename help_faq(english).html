Frequently Asked Questions

What is Invoice Kilat?

Invoice Kilat is online software that can help you create invoices, store information about clients (customer) and products item.

What are the benefits I get by using the Invoice Kilat service?

Users (sellers) can rapidly plan billings to customers without studying complex accounting and wasting longer time. Users can do it anywhere.

How do I create my first invoice?

You can read the user manual describing the steps to create invoices to the customer.

Can I use Invoice Kilat without login?

You can use Invoice Kilat Free feature without login first.

Is the Invoice Kilat Free really free?

You can use Invoice Kilat Free for free at no charge and can print directly.

Does Invoice Kilat have several different templates to use?

Invoice Kilat is currently available in one tamplate. But in its development can be increased.

Can I use Invoice Kilat if I only have some customers?

You can still use Invoice Kilat no matter how many customers you have.

How to send invoice to customer's email?

You can send invoice to customer, by:
  1. Open the invoice details you want to send.
  2. Click Sent (envelope icon) at the top left of the invoice details.
  3. Enter information (such as: destination address, title, message) as needed.
  4. Click Send to send to the destination email.

Can I change the price on the invoice without changing the actual price of the product?

You can change the different price as long as the invoice is still a draft, by:
  1. Create an invoice as usual by first entering the invoice and due date, selecting the customer, and adding the product.
  2. Product details will be displayed and you can change the price of the product by changing it in the Rate column.
  3. After changing as needed you can save it again.

How do I update my payment status?

You only need to open the invoice details that will be changed status. Then click Save and Pay when it's paid.

What payment methods are available?

You can choose the method of payment through cash or bank transfer.
