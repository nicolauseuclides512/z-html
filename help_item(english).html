Items

Add Item

  1. Click Items on the left side of the screen, then click New at the top right of the screen.
  2. Enter product information next to each column. You must fill in the product name and price.
  3. Click Save to save the produk item.

Edit Item

  1. Go to the list of product items by clicking Items on the left side of the screen.
  2. Click the item you want to change.
  3. Change as you need. The name and price of the item must remain filled.
  4. Click Save to save your changes.

Delete Item

  1. Open the product tem list by clicking Items on the left side of the screen.
  2. Check the small box for the item you want to delete.
  3. Click More Action at the top of the screen, then click Delete.
  4. The screen will display a confirmation message if you are sure you will delete the item, if yes click Delete.
  5. Item will be deleted.
