Sales Orders

Create Sales Order

  1. Click + on Sales Order on the left side of the screen.
  2. Enter the bill date and due in the available fields. If you do not enter the due date then it will automatically equal the bill date.
  3. Select the customer you want to make the bill.
  4. The invoice format will be displayed and you can add the product you want to add to the invoice list.
  5. You can add the amount in each product you enter. You can also change the price of the product, add a discount and adjustments to your liking.
  6. The system will automatically accumulate all normal totals automatically.
  7. If you want to give a message to the customer, you can add to the available fields.
  8. Save the invoice you have created.
    • Save as Draf - Save the invoice as a draft.
    • Save then Close - Save the invoice.
    • Save then Pay - Save the invoice already paid by the customer.
    • Save and New - Save the invoice.
    • Save and Duplicate - Save duplicate invoices.

Edit Sales Order

  1. The sales order that has been made can be edited as long as it is still stored as a draft and unpaid status.
  2. Open the sales order details you want to change, then click Edit at the top right of the screen.
  3. You can edit invoices as needed.
  4. Save the sales order you have edited.

Membatalkan Sales Order

  1. Sales order yang sudah dibuat bisa dibatalkan selama masih tersimpan sebagai draf dan berstatus unpaid.
  2. Buka detail sales order yang ingin kamu batalkan, lalu klik Void di bagian kanan atas layar.
  3. Sales order telah dibatalkan.

Memfilter Tampilan Sales Order

  1. Kamu bisa memfilter sales order berdasarkan statusnya.
  2. Buka halaman sales order dengan mengklik Sales Order di bagian kiri layar.
  3. Setelah daftar sales order ditampilkan, klik All di bagian atas layar. Kamu bisa memilih status sales order yang ingin kamu tampilkan.
  4. Layar akan menampilan sales order berdasarkan status yang sudah kamu pilih.
