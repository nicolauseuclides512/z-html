Contacts

Add Contact (Customer)

  1. Click Contacts on the left side of the screen, then click New on the top right of the screen.
  2. Enter customer information in each column.
  3. You can add addresses and records about the customer.
  4. Click Save to saved customer contact.

Edit Contact (Customer)

  1. Open the product customer list by clicking Contacts on the left side of the screen.
  2. Click the contact you want to change.
  3. Change as you need. The name and price of the item must remain filled.
  4. Click Save to save your changes.

Delete Contact (Customer)

  1. Open the product tem list by clicking Contacts on the left side of the screen.
  2. Check the small box for the contact you want to delete.
  3. Click More Action at the top of the screen, then click Delete .
  4. LThe screen will display a confirmation message if you are sure you will delete the item, if yes click Delete.
  5. Contact (customer) has been deleted.
