General Guidlines

Register New Account

  1. Click DAFTAR on the top right of the screen.
  2. Fill in the information as requested for each column.
  3. Do not forget to read the terms and conditions of the Quick Invoice through the link at the bottom of the registration form. If you agree, check the small box next to the approval statement.
  4. Click Register.
  5. After registering, we will send an email to verify the email account you are using. Click the VERIFY NOW button in that email to verify your account.

Masuk (Login)

  1. Log in to the login page by clicking ENTER at the top right of the screen.
  2. Enter the email and password you registered.
  3. If you want to always sign in automatically without entering your email and password, check the little box next to Remember me.

Forget Password

  1. Log in to the login page by clicking ENTER at the top right of the screen.
  2. Click Forget Your Password at the bottom of the login form.
  3. Enter the email you registered, then click Reset.
  4. We will send you an email to reset your password. Click the RESET PASSWORD button in the email to reset your account password.
  5. Enter your new email and password information, then click Reset Password.
  6. You can now login with your new password.

Change Password

  1. Click on your email at the top right, then click Account.
  2. Enter the old password and new password in each column.
  3. Click Save to save password changes.

Exit (Logout)

  1. Click on your email at the top right, then click Logout.
  2. You have logged out of your account.
