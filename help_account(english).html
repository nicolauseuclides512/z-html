Manage Accounts

Company Profile

  1. Click Settings on the left side of the screen, then click Company Profile.
  2. Here you can do two things.
  3. First, you can enter a logo photo by clicking on the available logo box.
  4. Secondly, you can enter the organization information in each of the available fields.
  5. Click Save to saved.

Store Details

  1. Click Settings on the left side of the screen, then click Store Details.
  2. You can enter local time information, currency, and language settings in the fields provided.
  3. Click Save to saved.

Payment Setting

  1. Click Settings on the left side of the screen, then click Payment Setting.
  2. You can choose a cash payment or with bank transfer.
  3. If you choose to pay by bank transfer, you are expected to enter the bank information you use. Click Add to add it.
  4. You can add multiple account numbers that you use.
